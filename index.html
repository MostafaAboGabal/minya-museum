About of the Faculty of Tourism and Hotels

was established by Presidential Decree No. 419 in 1995 and studies actually began in the academic year 1997/1998. The Faculty of Tourism and Hotels is considered one of the oldest colleges in Upper Egypt, as it is one of the first tourism and hotel colleges concerned with teaching tourism and hospitality sciences to the North, Central and South Upper Egypt sectors. It obtained academic accreditation to ensure the quality of education and accreditation in May 2018. The college awards a bachelor's degree and the duration of study is four years. The college includes three departments (tourism guidance, tourism studies, hotel management) in addition to the distinguished program that was added in 2021/2022, the Tourism and Hospitality Industry Technology Program
